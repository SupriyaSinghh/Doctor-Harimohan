I belong to 'Sanha parora' village of 'Begusrai'. My father used to be a farmer. I saw a large number of people in the village dying out of shortage of basic health facilities. But the death of my garandpa due to the same reason hurted me deep inside and that day only I dcided to be a doctor to serve the people of Bihar. The economical condition was a great hinderance in doing so, the cost of medical coaching was creating a cemented wall in the way of my goal. But if you do something with a good intention to help the mankind, collective blessings work for you in behind. That happened to me as well and I completed my medical journey with 5 gold medals on the chest making all the obstacles a history.
